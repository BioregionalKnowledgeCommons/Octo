## Why Octo

The octopus is the model of distributed intelligence ‚Äî nine brains working in concert, each arm sensing and responding to its own environment while the central brain maintains coherence. No rigid hierarchy, no single point of failure. Intelligence distributed to the edges, where the contact with the world actually happens.

This is how a bioregion coordinates. This is how a knowledge commons works. This is how re/acc scales ‚Äî many centers, one living web.

And yes ‚Äî OpenClaw is a lobster. Octo is an octopus. Sea creatures, both. But where the lobster has claws that grip and execute, the octopus has arms that sense, taste, and feel. Eight of them, simultaneously. Perception as a way of being.
